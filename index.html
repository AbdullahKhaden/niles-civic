NDAA, Sec. 219 Urgent

Stop Section 219 in the NDAA

Section 219 of the FY2027 NDAA (H.R. 8800) would formalize a U.S. and Israel Defense Technology Cooperation Initiative across cyber, AI, missile defense, and quantum systems. A bipartisan amendment to strip it was blocked from a floor vote. It's now stuck in reconciliation between the House and Senate versions.
